Call Centre
For both OmniPCX Office & Enterprise

Vurella’s call centre solution provides a fully featured ACD package, including a wallboard to display live statistics for both inbound calls and the state of each agent. A supervisor application is also included and enables a range of reports to be generated on the fly or periodically.

With Vurella’s call centre, agents can now also work remotely using Rainbow on a PC or even via their smartphone to accept calls.

Features
Call Recording
VoIP, SIP, ISDN, Analog Recording

Vurella’s call recording solutions have everything your client needs to safety record and store calls. Whether your client has analogue lines or SIP trunks, we can record it.

Importantly, all our call recording solutions include pause & resume, encryption, call archiving and deletion, as well as a host of other features to ensure your clients can adhere to both PCI and GDPR data privacy policies.

Features
CTI Solutions
CRM Connectors, Call Logging, Click to Dial

vSUITE also includes a range of CTI features that ensure no box is left unticked. This Includes a call logging package, click to dial, a range of pre-built CRM connectors and 3rd party integration options.

In addition, where required these off the shelf features can also be customised to fit your clients requirements, ensuring you can offer a seamless solution.

Applications & Integration

Call Centre Agent

Vurella’s Rainbow agent is available on PC or Mobile and provides agents with the freedom to work from home. Despite the agent working externally, the supervisor can still monitor the agents presence, and what’s more, the agent does not need a VPN, just a standard internet connection.

Busy Lamp Field

Our busy lamp field provides users with the ability to monitor extensions company wide in order to see when their colleagues are busy or available. In addition, the user can also accept & transfer inbound calls by filtering their group contacts and then dragging and dropping the call onto the appropriate extension, much like a mini operator console.
